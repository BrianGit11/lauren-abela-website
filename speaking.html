Signature Talks

Four talks. One message: you're not alone.

🌿

The Power of Belonging

What does inclusion actually look like? Not the policy — the practice. Lauren shares the story of co-founding UofGH's first Best Buddies chapter and what she learned about what it means to truly make room for someone.

Best for: DEI events, disability advocacy conferences, student leadership, university assemblies

🔨

Build What Doesn't Exist Yet

The story of what happens when you see a problem, look around for someone to fix it, and realize it might have to be you. A talk on leadership, courage, and the moment you stop waiting for permission.

Best for: Student leadership conferences, graduation ceremonies, emerging leader programs

🤝

The Friendship Effect

Friendship is the foundation of every community worth being part of — but we underestimate it constantly. Lauren unpacks what real friendship across difference looks like and why it changes everything for both people involved.

Best for: High school assemblies, Best Buddies events, community organizations

📚

You Can Do This

For students who wonder if they're smart enough, ready enough, or good enough. A talk on navigating academic life, asking for help without shame, and what it looks like to succeed when the path isn't straight.

Best for: College orientations, academic support programs, high school seniors

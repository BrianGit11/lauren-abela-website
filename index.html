Lauren's Story

She saw a gap.
And she didn't fill it alone.

"We want people to believe that they can do anything, they have a community — and to know that we have their backs."

When Lauren arrived at the University of Guelph-Humber, there was no Best Buddies chapter on campus. She recognized the need, reached out to her friend Jessica Rotolo, and together they built one from the ground up. They won Canada's inaugural Ambassador of Change Award for it. Then Best Buddies International named Lauren and her buddy Global Ambassadors for 2023. The first Canadian buddy pair in the program's history to earn that title.
